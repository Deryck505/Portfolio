Our projects

We have some of the many projects we have successfully completed:

  1. Residential Lighting Upgrade: Almasi Electrical transformed a home's lighting system, enhancing both functionality and aesthetics. By installing energy-efficient LED lights, dimmer switches, and smart lighting controls, we created a personalized and efficient lighting experience for the homeowner.
  2. Home Automation Integration: Almasi Electrical seamlessly integrated a comprehensive home automation system into a client's residence. This project involved connecting various devices, such as lighting, HVAC, security systems, and entertainment systems, to a centralized control system, allowing the homeowner to manage and monitor their home with ease.
  3. Electrical Panel Upgrade: Almasi Electrical undertook a project to upgrade an outdated electrical panel in a residential property. Our team replaced the old panel with a modern, higher-capacity panel, ensuring safe and reliable power distribution throughout the home.
  4. Outdoor Landscape Lighting: Almasi Electrical designed and installed a stunning outdoor landscape lighting system for a client's property. This project involved strategically placing lights to highlight architectural features, illuminate pathways, and create a captivating ambiance in the outdoor space.
  5. Home Security System Installation: Almasi Electrical implemented a comprehensive home security system for a client, including surveillance cameras, motion sensors, and smart locks. This project aimed to provide the homeowner with peace of mind and enhanced security measures to protect their property.

These projects showcase Almasi Electrical's expertise in delivering customized electrical solutions that enhance the comfort, convenience, and safety of residential spaces.
